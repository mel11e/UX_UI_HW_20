The Problem

Curated was designed to achieve a new way for users to discover new artists and to stay up-to-date on events, news, and trends. We have observed that art enthusiasts are not feeling connected to the arts community, which is causing users to miss out on artist’s events and news. How might we improve an art enthusiast’s awareness of trends, local events, while generating a customized list of preferred artists?

The Solution

To solve the problem a mobile app was created to help art seeking individuals connect with local artists, find events, learn about art, and have a customized usability experience with the app using AI. Research was conducted to identify goals and pain points which art enthusiasts experience frequently.

The Process

01

Research

To begin the investiagtion, we created a questionnaire using Survey Monkey to gather qualitative data and conducted five one-on-one user interviews online over Zoom. Both questionnaire and user interviews focused mainly on the users level of art knowledge; art trends and news; reasons, purpose, and goals for attending art exhibits and museums. While other questions were asked to collect demographic data for our user persona. We also investiaged traveling pain points and preference UI interactions while using a mobile app. We also conducted a competitor analysis to find strentghs and weakness.

User Demographics
Key Findings
  • The majority of participants want to be updated on the latest news about art.
  • All participants expressed frustration when they missed an art exhibit, because they had no knowledge of it until the exhibit expired.
  • Favorite app features include: ability to identify art, learn about the artists, locate art events, receive suggestions, and alerts about upcoming art events.
  • All participants want to learn more about artists.
  • Majority of participants are Apple mobile users.

    Storyboard
    User Scenario

    James is on a business trip in New York City and has some free time in between meetings. He wants to visit a local museum, but he’s not sure where to start looking. He doesn’t like using main stream social media for searching. He messages a colleague, knowing she’s worked in the city before, to see if she has suggestions. She tells him about a local museum in the area. James searches for the museum using an app and find the website. He purchases one ticket. He uses a different app to help him locate the museum. Once he is at the museum, James wants to learn more about a painting he sees and about the artist. He using a different app to search the name of the painting. After the museum visit he leaves a review.

    User Goal

    James wants to find a local museum exhibit, but he is unfamiliar with his surroundings on his business trip. He downloads the app CURATED recommended by a colleague. James purchases a ticket on the app. He arrives at the museum and sees a painting he wants to learn more about. He uses the image recognition option of CURATED to pull up information about the painting and the artist.

    User Flow

    Gathering the results of our feature prioritization matrix and discovering the oppoutunities from the Journey Map we identified key features to use in our mobile design.

    Main Features

  • Create an AI gamified quiz to help users have a personalized experience.
  • Add an art scan feature to increase art knowledge while at an exhibit.
  • Connect traveling art enthusiasts to art events by allowing users to purchasing tickets on the app.

    • The user flow created displays a user's complete path to engage with our art quiz, use the art scanning feature, as well as locate local art events.

    05

    Testing

    We tested our mobile prototype on five users. We hoped to learn what areas of our designs were seamless as well as where we can improve user flow. Our testing focused on:

  • The onboarding process
  • User understanding how the user profile quiz functions.
  • Search for a museum or art gallery.
  • Purchase tickets.
  • Search for an artist.
  • Find and use the art image scanning recognition feature.
    • Usability Testing Results

    Our results showed that the onboarding process was seamless, yet one user experssed a desire to know why the app was asking for phone GPS access. When we tested our profile quiz, there was a lack of intuitive understanding when using the quiz. Some users committed that they need more of an explanation or tutorial on how to use the quiz before starting. Our last task of purchasing a ticket was easy for most of our users. However, one user committed that our naviagation bar looked similar to Instgram.

    06

    Conclusion

    Key Take Aways

    Pain Points for art enthusiasts include:

  • Dislike missing art exhibits.
  • Does not know where to find a specific artist’s work.
  • A knowledge gap existing in art styles and movements.
  • Lack of information about new artists, trends, and news.

    • CURATED seeks to solves this by connecting users to:

  • Information about budding artists.
  • Details about an artist’s style and their artwork.
  • Knowledge about different art styles and movements.
  • Current art exhibit events.
  • Receive information about latest art trends and news.
